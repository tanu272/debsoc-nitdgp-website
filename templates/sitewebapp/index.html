About us

The Debating Society, NIT Durgapur was founded in 2015 with the vision of promoting a healthy debating culture among the college students and has never looked back since then. From conducting intra college debates to the recently conducted Asian Parliamentary Debate that saw participation from various schools and colleges from across the city, the club has come a long way and has built a reputation for itself. We represent NIT Durgapur in various debating competitions across the country and has carved a niche for itself in the Eastern and Central debating circuits.
